2. Do you want to become a Miituber?

-Do you have a YouTube channel and think it could become a MiiTube channel? Then follow these steps!

-1. Check if your channel meets the following criterias:
1. Your channel must have published at least one LONG video (>=1)
2. almost all/all videos on your channel must be related to mii, Wii, WiiU, 3DS etc...
3. (optional) Your channel should have a picture of your mii as your profile picture
4. Your channel must have quality content:
Your channel must not violate YouTube's community guidelines
Your channel must not be a reupload channel (channels that reupload other people's content without permission)
Your channel must not be a spam channel (channels that post repetitive or irrelevant content)
Your channel must not contain inappropriate content (18+)
The videos you upload to your channel must have been created with effort (e.g., not low quality AI-generated content, or content you created in a short time)
NO BRAINROT CONTENT
-2. Contact me!:
Contact me by sending me
1. your channel's profile picture
2. your YouTube channel name
3. a brief description of your channel
4. your channel's link address
I will then reply to you letting you know if your channel has been accepted.
